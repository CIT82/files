### Week 3 Thursday: Learn Basic CSS - Theory & Cafe Menu

In this assignment, you transition from structure (HTML) to style (CSS). This requires completing the foundational theory and starting your first styled project.

#### Part 1: freeCodeCamp Workshop
1. Log into your account at [freecodecamp.org](https://www.freecodecamp.org).
2. Complete all **10 sections** of the **"What is CSS"** theory module.
3. Complete the first **20 steps** of the **"Learn Basic CSS by Building a Cafe Menu"** workshop.
4. **Capture the Proof:** Take a screenshot that shows **BOTH**:
   - The checkmarks/completion status for all 10 "What is CSS" sections.
   - Progress showing you are on Step 20 of the Cafe Menu.
   - **IMPORTANT:** Your screenshot **MUST** include your account photo/profile icon in the top right corner.

#### Part 2: Asset Management & Organization
1. **Resize:** Resize your screenshot to a width of **500px** (maintain aspect ratio) using [Squoosh.app](https://squoosh.app/).
2. **Rename:** Save this file as `w3p4.png` (or `.jpg` / `.webp`).
3. **Place:** Move `w3p4.png` into your private repo at `lectures/freecodecamp/img/`.

#### Part 3: Update index.html (Topic Separation)
1. Open your `index.html` in the `freecodecamp` folder.
2. **HTML Section:** Ensure you have an `<h2>HTML</h2>` heading above your previous links.
3. **CSS Section:** Below your HTML links, add an `<h2>CSS</h2>` heading.
4. **Add Link:** Under the CSS heading, add an anchor tag (`<a>`) for this assignment.
   - **Example:** `<a href="img/w3p4.png">View my CSS Theory & Cafe Menu (Steps 1-20)</a>`
