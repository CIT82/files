### Week 3 Part 3: Building the Cat Photo App

In this assignment, you will continue your work on freeCodeCamp. This workshop is more intensive (42 steps), focusing on the most common HTML elements you will use as a web developer.

#### Part 1: freeCodeCamp Workshop
1. Log into your account at [freecodecamp.org](https://www.freecodecamp.org).
2. Complete the **"Learn HTML by Building a Cat Photo App"** workshop (42 steps).
3. **Capture:** Take a screenshot of your completed work.
   - **IMPORTANT:** Your screenshot **MUST** include your account photo/profile icon in the top right corner for verification.

#### Part 2: Asset Management & Organization
1. **Resize:** Resize your screenshot to a width of **500px** (maintain aspect ratio) using [Squoosh.app](https://squoosh.app/) or your preferred tool.
2. **Rename:** Save this file as `w3p3.png` (or `.jpg` / `.webp`).
3. **Place:** Move `w3p3.png` into your private repo at `lectures/freecodecamp/img/`.

#### Part 3: Update index.html
1. Open your existing `index.html` in the `freecodecamp` folder.
2. **Add Link:** Below your first link, add a new anchor tag (`<a>`) that points to this new screenshot.
   - **Example:** `<a href="img/w3p3.png">View my Cat Photo App Completion</a>`
